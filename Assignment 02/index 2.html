WHAT THIS IS

October Fest is an annual Volksfest (folks festival) in which around six million people attend in Munich Germany. It is usually held around mid-September till the first couple of days in October. Many of the attractions that bring people is the Beer, Wurst, and the Good Times.


WHEN

2017: September 16th - October 3rd


COST

October Fest is free to attend. The only cost is for what you eat, drink and play.

WHAT TO TRY

WEISS WURST: It's a white sausage that can be paired well with mustard and beer
PRETZLES: Pretzles are pretty common in Germany, so you have to expect it in the largets celebration. Pretzles are usually served warm and paired with mustard.
BEER: There are hundreds of beers to try. Just try them, as many as you can.
